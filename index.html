Javascript

Not to be confused with Java or a scripting language. Javascript was created after Java and carries a similar name but the similarities end there. It runs in browsers and on servers with nodejs. Javascript is also one of the hated langs. Even as a normie you can understand that issue just right click anywhere in your browser to open the right click menu > inspect > console. Then type   "11" + 1    in the console, hit enter and you would see the answer... which is kinda correct. Now   "11" - 1   ... you see the glaring issue? not even the worst of it..Even as a normie you can understand that issue..   "11" + 1 = "111"   okayish yea? js joined them and wrote them together, but then   "11" - 1 = 10  .. Js really really tries not to give you an error so it actually just substracts it. which shouldn't happen..
